About Us

Our Roots In Kindness (Origin Story) Kindness is at the heart of everything we do – but – we’re about so much more. With the aim of creating skincare that is fuss-free and approachable, we’re committed to keeping you and your skin looking (and feeling!) its best, starting with clean, uncomplicated products that downright love your skin and the planet too. What is clean beauty, you ask? It is saying a big NO to baddies such as perfume/fragrance, colours, etc known to upset your skin, because what we leave out is just as important as what we put in. What’s even more amazing is that Simple has been in the clean beauty game way before it was cool!
